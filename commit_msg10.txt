Optimize invalidateRecording to skip redundant snapshot when timeline is fresh

TickController.invalidateRecording: skip resetRecording (expensive full
workspace snapshot) when the timeline exists but has zero deltas,
meaning freeze() just created a fresh recording. This avoids double
snapshot capture when inputs are applied immediately after freezing,
improving test suite performance proportional to workspace size.
